RSCs

The App router from Next.js introduced a lot of weird rules, for example, around caching and not allowing partial page updates. But they have nothing to do with RSC and everything to do with Next.js. There are actually very few rules when it comes to RSC, as you can see with the Navigation router. All the Navigation router documentation, apart from the setup, works the same with or without RSC.
